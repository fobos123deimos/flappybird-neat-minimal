This directory contains a customized version of the PyGame Learning Environment (PLE),
originally developed by Nuno Tasfi and contributors:
https://github.com/ntasfi/PyGame-Learning-Environment

The original project is licensed under the MIT License.

Modifications were made in 2024 by Matheus Gomes Cordeiro to enable controlled
scenario generation and better integration with NEAT-based training strategies
for the Flappy Bird game environment.

These modifications were made for research and experimentation purposes.
